# Be Brief, Brilliant, Gone!

## Description

Be brief. Be brilliant. Be gone.

That's what I always coach people on.

A short video forces you to distill it down to ONE thing: What is the key message I want to communicate to this person?

No 3-minute waffling. Trust me.. I've done that. It doesn't work. Nobody's watching you ramble.

Keep it under a minute. Honestly, even at 1:01, people are like "ah, too long for me."

Here's why short works:

It gets you OUT of talking about features. Out of "here's how great our company is." And INTO what actually matters—what is gonna pattern interrupt for this person? What are they thinking about in their day-to-day?

"Hey, are you thinking about filling this open role?"
"Hey, are you thinking about building a pathway for retention?"

If you can connect with THAT, you can talk about features later.

You're trying to get their attention in under 30 seconds. That forces you to be crystal clear on what THEY care about.

Not a laundry list of everything you can do.

Brief. Brilliant. Gone.
