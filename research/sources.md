## How Experts Were Selected
Experts were chosen based on three criteria: active sharing of cold outreach 
tactics grounded in real execution, direct experience building B2B SaaS 
pipelines, and evidence of practice over theory (live calls, real frameworks, 
case studies with actual numbers). Each expert brings a distinct angle on the 
cold outreach pipeline — from messaging psychology to outbound systems to 
pipeline progression.
