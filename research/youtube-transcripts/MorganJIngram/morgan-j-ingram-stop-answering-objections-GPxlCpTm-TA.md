# Stop Answering Objections

- **Publish date**: 2026-04-15T10:00:25Z
- **URL**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GPxlCpTm-TA

## Description

Most reps keep talking until they kill the deal. There's a psychological limit to how many objections you should ever answer on a cold call, and crossing it costs you the meeting every time.
