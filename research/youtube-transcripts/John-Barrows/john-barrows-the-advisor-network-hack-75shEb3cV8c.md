## Description

The fastest way to get in front of a C-suite isn't cold outreach. It's a warm introduction from someone they already trust.

Eric Appel reveals how Island built a network of 70+ advisors — former executives, retired CISOs, and industry insiders — to generate a pipeline at scale without relying on traditional prospecting. The two-way structure meant advisors weren't just opening doors. They were improving the product.

The right network doesn't just generate leads. It builds the business.

How Island cracked the enterprise pipeline is broken down in this episode of the Make It Happen Mondays Podcast.
