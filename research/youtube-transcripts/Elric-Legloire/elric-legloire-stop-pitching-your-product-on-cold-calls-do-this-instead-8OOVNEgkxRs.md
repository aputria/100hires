# Stop Pitching Your Product on Cold Calls (Do This Instead)

- **Publish date**: 2026-02-02T20:51:43Z
- **URL**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8OOVNEgkxRs

## Description

Problem-based language converts 3x better than product pitching.

That's not opinion. That's Gong data from 100,000+ cold calls.

16% success rate when you talk about problems.
5.5% when you lead with product buzzwords.

@Jason Bay calls this the "reverse pitch."

Instead of: "Hey, at XYZ company we help businesses do ABC..."

Focus on: "The number one thing we're hearing from leaders like you is [specific problem]. Is that something you're thinking about?"

Most reps get solution-focused too early. They talk product before they've earned the right.

The reverse pitch flips that. Lead with problems. Let them ask what you do.
