## Post 4

- **Date:** March 2026
- **Engagement:** 39 likes · 17 comments
- **Post URL:** [View on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/elriclegloire_my-take-on-the-gtm-engineers-replacing-sdrs-activity-7434237143536795648-AbNx)
- **Why it matters:** Data-backed counter to the "SDRs are dead" narrative — offers a nuanced integrated strategy instead

My take on the "GTM Engineers replacing SDRs" trend: 

It's dangerously oversimplified and misaligned with outbound reality.

Today, I just read ANOTHER post claiming " the old way of SDRs is dead, GTM Engineers are the future of scaling outbound."

In February alone, there were 14,135 SDR job openings globally. 
GTM Engineer openings? 317. 
Total GTM Engineers on LinkedIn? Around 2,125.

SDRs aren't going anywhere. 

But companies blindly chasing the "AI-only" trend are making massive strategic mistakes.

Here are the 2 biggest things the "SDR is dead" crowd is missing:

1. GTM Engineers aren't just for cold outreach.

The narrative paints GTM Engineers purely as top-of-funnel outbound automation machines. In reality, a GTM Engineer working full-time at a SaaS company impacts the full revenue cycle, from inbound routing and enrichment to CRM hygiene and customer lifecycle ops. Confining them to just cold email is a waste of their talent. 

1. Effective outbound is dictated by your market.

This dangerous "either/or" mindset pushes teams into bad strategies:

- GTM Engineers plateau fast with complex enterprise accounts or blue collar industries.
- Programmatic outbound relies heavily on email, but scaling still demands phone outreach.
- For high ACV/LTV accounts, pipeline quality matters far more than low CAC.

SDRs without ops support waste hours on manual tasks, but AI-only outreach consistently underperforms human-led prospecting in these tiers.

The truth is, it's not "SDRs OR GTM Engineers." It's how you integrate BOTH.

Launching outbound?

- SMB / Mid-Market, white-collar, lower LTV: Let GTM Engineers build the email machine. Let SDRs handle replies and calls.
- SMB / Mid-Market, blue-collar or service businesses: Make the motion call-first. SDRs run the calls. GTM Engineers build the system behind it.
- Enterprise, high LTV: Put SDRs at the center of outreach across at least 3 channels. Use GTM Engineers to support them.

Already established?
Deploy GTM Engineers to empower your SDRs on Tier 1 accounts, not replace them.
Leverage GTM Engineers for automated coverage of Tier 2/3 accounts that would otherwise go untouched.

Top outbound teams in 2026 aren't using tech to eliminate SDRs; they are investing in tech to make their SDRs 3-4x more efficient. 

Stop buying into the either/or hype. Build integrated strategies that reflect business reality.
